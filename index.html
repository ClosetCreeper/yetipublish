Emoji Picker & Auto Emoji

Browse a curated catalog with Discord-style shortcodes, or skip the picker entirely — Auto Emoji types the shortcode straight into whatever you're writing.

  • Arrow keys + Return to copy
  • Auto Emoji works system-wide via a low-level event tap
  • Reliable even in browser text fields

Bookmarks

Save a link once by name and URL. Search it from the launcher, or turn on Auto Replace and just type the name anywhere — Yeti drops in the full link.

  • Auto-formats bare URLs (adds https://)
  • Return opens directly in your browser
  • Auto Replace works outside the launcher too
